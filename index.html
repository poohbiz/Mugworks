How We Make Them

Design

Sketches, ergonomics, and balance

We iterate handle curvature, wall thickness, and center of mass so the mug feels right in the hand—light enough to lift, sturdy enough to last.

Glaze

Warm seasonal finishes

Small-batch color testing for those cozy caramel/latte tones, fired to food-safe standards with consistent schedules for reliable color.

Kiln

High-fire for durability

High-fire cycle for strength—dishwasher/microwave friendly for daily use. Each batch is inspected micro-cracks and glaze integrity.
